Dec. 20th

Secret Santa gift exchange

Members of SADC would be given random names to become someone’s Secret Santa. The Secret Santa is given 5 different descriptions of a desired gift ($15 budget) to give to their chosen giftee. 

How to play:

1. An email will be send to all SADC team members and each person would have to write 5 different descriptions of their desired gift ($15 budget)  in “my wish list” using the link provided in the email from drawnames@drawnames.com.

2. A second email will be send to inform you with your giftee’s description of his/her desired gift.

3. Attach the 5 description and place it on the top of the Christmas gift.

4. All participants will have to leave their secret Santa gift under the tree

5. Participant will be picked randomly to guess what’s the gift (based on the description) and who it’s for.

6. The recipient will reveal himself and open the gift.

Dec. 20th

Sell your Christmas Gift

Members of SADC would have to prepare a random wrapped $5 Christmas gift.  

Each member has to present and “sell” their unknown Christmas gift in hopes of getting a better present. This game will be played for 2-3 rounds where the participants have to trade the unknown gift. 

Tip: Wrap your gift differently to make it harder for other players to guess.
